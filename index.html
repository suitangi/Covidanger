The R₀ Value
Scientists use R₀ to measure the intensity of an outbreak. It is the
average number of people an infected person will spread the disease to.
The common flu has an R₀ of 1.3
Covid-19 has an R₀ of 2 to 2.2
These numbers seem close, but in reality, it's big difference.
